HERITAGE // EST. 1970

A Legacy of Innovation

Founded as a European consortium, Airbus revolutionized the aviation market by creating wide-body twin-engine aircraft and the iconic A380 double-decker. Today, it stands as the vanguard of sustainable aerospace solutions.

FUTURE // HYDROGEN PROPULSION

The ZEROe Initiative

Looking to the future, Airbus is developing ZEROe, the world's first zero-emission commercial aircraft concept. Utilizing hydrogen propulsion, these revolutionary designs aim to enter service by 2035.

// ZEROE PROGRAM

Flying on Hydrogen

The ZEROe aircraft family explores three revolutionary concepts: a turbofan, a turboprop, and a blended-wing body — all powered by cryogenic liquid hydrogen stored in tanks behind the rear pressure bulkhead.

The combustion of hydrogen produces water vapor as its only byproduct, creating a truly emissions-free flight experience at commercial scale.
