// ARIANE 6 LAUNCH VEHICLE

Next-Generation Access to Space

The Ariane 6 rocket is Europe's primary heavy-lift launch vehicle, capable of delivering payloads up to 21.6 tonnes to low Earth orbit or 11.5 tonnes to geostationary transfer orbit.

Launched from the Guiana Space Centre, Ariane 6 features a versatile upper stage that can be restarted multiple times — enabling multi-orbit missions and satellite constellation deployment in a single flight.
