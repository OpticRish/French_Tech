# Innovative France — Technology & Engineering Portal

A premium, interactive web experience showcasing 400 years of French technological leadership and futuristic engineering.


## 🚀 Overview

**Innovative France** is a high-end digital hub designed to celebrate the engineering marvels that define the French spirit of discovery. From the wide-body twin-engine logic of **Airbus** and the record-breaking speed of the **TGV**, to the tactical air superiority of the **Dassault Rafale** and the galactic reach of **Arianespace**, this portal provides an immersive deep dive into the nation's technological DNA.

## ✨ Core Features

### ✈️ Sector Spotlights
- **Airbus & ZeroE**: Detailed dossiers on European aviation leadership and the hydrogen-powered future of flight.
- **Arianespace**: An animated space exploration module featuring Ariane 6 launch vehicle specs and mission logs.
- **Dassault Rafale**: A cinematic carousel showcase of the "Omnirole" fighter jet with high-performance flight footage.
- **SNCF TGV**: Interactive speed benchmarks and route visualizations for the world's most iconic high-speed train.

### ⏳ Innovation Timeline
- A chronological "Mind Tree" of history's greatest French inventions.
- Dedicated standalone articles for high-impact milestones: *The Metric System, Pasclaline, Radioactivity, The Parachute, and more.*

### 🛠️ Advanced User Experience
- **Premium Glassmorphic UI**: A dark-mode first design utilizing backdrop filters and modern CSS typography.
- **Custom Jet Cursor**: A dynamic Rafale-themed cursor with afterburner effects and aerodynamic cloud trails.
- **Scroll Progress Tracking**: A persistent, neon-gradient progress indicator for seamless navigation.
- **Bilingual Support**: Fully localized in English and French.

## 💻 Technology Stack

- **Foundations**: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+), CSS3
- **Animations**: GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) for high-performance motion, CSS Keyframes for micro-animations.
- **Design Systems**: Custom CSS variables for theme management, FontAwesome for iconography, Google Fonts (Outfit & Space Mono).
- **Architecture**: Modular JavaScript structure with centralized localization and component-based navigation.
